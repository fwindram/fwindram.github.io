About Me

I am a computational ecologist interested in imaging biology, mechanistic modelling, and spider web traits.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Imperial College London, working on the One Health Vector-Borne Diseases Hub. Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor's in Zoology at the University of Leeds followed by my Master's degree in Computational Methods in Ecology and Evolution (CMEE) and PhD at Imperial College London.
